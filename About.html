Our Story

How We Started

Rakspark Academy was founded in 2025 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality JAMB preparation accessible to every Nigerian student, regardless of their location or economic background. What started as a small tutorial center in Lagos has grown into Nigeria's leading online JAMB preparation platform, serving over 50,000 students nationwide.

Our founder, Dr. Rakesh Adeyemi, a first-class graduate of Computer Science from the University of Lagos, experienced firsthand the challenges students face in preparing for JAMB. After scoring 356 in his own JAMB examination, he realized that with the right resources, guidance, and technology, every student could achieve exceptional results.

Today, Rakspark Academy combines cutting-edge technology with proven teaching methodologies to deliver comprehensive JAMB preparation that produces consistent results. Our platform has helped thousands of students gain admission to their dream universities, with an average student score improvement of over 80 points.

We believe that every student deserves access to world-class education, and we're committed to breaking down barriers to academic success across Nigeria.
